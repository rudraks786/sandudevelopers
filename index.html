About us

We come from a century old legacy of trust in the Ayurveda space, before venturing into the real estate space with Sandu Developers. For us, it’s not just a ‘house’ we’re building, it’s a ‘home’ for a lifetime. This philosophy translates to our unmatched expertise and passionate approach in every aspect of real estate. While being deep-rooted in tradition, we’ve continually evolved to bring about graceful elevations with innovative technology, and contemporary design. Our company comes with over a century worth of trust and business expertise, and as we carry forward the mantle in the real estate sector, we are committed to bring you nothing but the best.
